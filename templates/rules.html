Rules

In order to participate in Origames you have to create a team of 9 people consisting METU Orienteering Team athletes. The fantasy team which collects the most points at the end of 2 days will be the winner of this game. Every manager has 100 Oricredits and each athlete have some Oricredit value to be paid.


Team Selection Criteria

Oricredits are the Origames currency. Every player is given 100 Oricredits to select their athletes. Each athlete has given a value in oricredits, based on their prospective points-scoring abilities. The oricredits values are based on past results and recent form, with an emphasis towards those athletes who have been successful in past editions of the event. Every player can select 5 elites, 3 veterans and has 1 joker selection. With the 1 joker option fantasy game player can select elite or veteran athlete.

Scoring

Scores will be obtained through the time points based on Turkish Orienteering Federation(TOF) formulation. The scores obtained from TOF will be multiplied by the following coefficients based on difficulty level of the categories.

Elites (Group 1): M21E, W21E, W55, M20A, W20A -> 1 1
Veterans (Group 2): M21A, W21A -> 0.85


Prizes

Entry fee is 3 TL for Origames Niksar Tour. Managers of the winner, runner-up and third teams will qualify for special prizes.
