Rules

In order to participate in Origames you have to create a team of 9 people consisting of METU Orienteering athletes. The team consisting of mixed athletes collects the most points at the end of 2 days will be the winner of this game. Every manager has 100 Oricredits and of you have to pay some credits to include each athlete to team.


Team Selection Criteria

Teams are given 100 Oricredits to select their athletes. Oricredits are the Origames currency. Each athlete is given a value in oricredits, based on their prospective points-scoring abilities. The oricredits values are based on past results and recent form, with an emphasis towards those athletes who have been successful in past editions of the event. You can select 3 elite, 4 veterans, 2 young athletes.

Scoring

Points will be obtained through the time points based on Turkish Orienteering Federation formulation. The scores obtained from Federation will be multiplied by the following coefficients based on difficulty level of the categories.

M21E, W21E, W20A -> 1
M21A, W21A -> 0.85
M20B, W20B, W21B, M21B, W55 -> 0.7
